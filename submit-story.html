Before You Share

Your story may be personal, emotional, painful, hopeful, simple or extraordinary. You decide how much you want to share and how it may be used.

🔒 You can stay anonymous.
📝 You can choose how much editing is allowed.
🎙 You can share text, audio, video or document links.
📚 You decide whether it may be used on the website, social media or books.
✉️ Your submission goes only to The Phoenix Whispers inbox first.

For audio/video/PDF files, upload your file to Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive or YouTube Unlisted, then paste the private link in the form.
